Hello!

Maps have always been a passion of mine, and will continue to be a part of my profession wherever I go. Before going back to school to obtain my Master's, I always wanted to find new ways and workflows that help deliver a project. Being at UW-Madison's program, I have been able to increase my skills and knowledge in map design, web map development, big data visualization, UX/UI design, geospatial database design, and R/Python scripting. Not only have I been able to get the most out of the classroom, but I have also taken the advantage of getting hands-experiences in the UW-Madison Department of Geography. Doing work with the State Cartographer's Office and Cartography Lab has helped implement project management experience, allowing me to expand my involvement more than just technical workflows.

When completed with this program, I hope to find a career that involves me to apply and learn more skills, while also being a part of a team that is always engaging in researching new ways to solve geographic phenomena. What I value about being in this field is how it is always evolving and changing, as I want to be with a group who always wants to be one step ahead.

While I make maps during the day, I like to spend my free time helping out and coach one of Madison's local swim clubs, or being outside with friends and family.

Thanks for checking out my web page! Feel free to connect with me with any questions/comments! My email is kwmcnair@wisc.edu.
