Naming Wisconsin

When looking at a modern map of Wisconsin, you will see so many names and places with several cultural backgrounds. I was more than excited to map out many of Wisconsin's place names from the French exploration and Native American names. Throughout this project, I tried to focus on the historical aesthetics of mapping during the 18th century, as well as to try and fit as much content as possible. A lot of careful research was used during the process, as I wanted to ensure the information being mapped was as up-to-date and accurate as possible.

Map was created using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop
